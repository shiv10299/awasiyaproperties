About This Property

This stunning 3BHK luxury villa is situated just 500 meters from the revered Banke Bihari Temple in Vrindavan. Built with premium materials and impeccable craftsmanship, it offers a rare opportunity to own a spiritual retreat that blends modern comfort with divine ambiance.

The property features a spacious living area with high ceilings, a modular kitchen with granite countertops, three fully air-conditioned bedrooms, and a private terrace overlooking the lush Braj landscape. The corner plot ensures maximum natural light and cross-ventilation throughout.

Located on Parikrama Marg, it offers easy access to all major temples, ghats, and markets. This is an ideal choice for devotees, retirees, or as a holiday home with excellent rental potential during pilgrimage seasons.
