🕉️ Our Story

Born in Braj, Built on Trust

Braj Properties was founded in 2000 by Shri Gopal Das Tripathi — a native of Mathura with a deep devotion to the land of Lord Krishna. What began as a small office near Yamuna Ghat has grown into the most trusted real estate firm in Braj Bhumi.

Over 25 years, we have helped thousands of families — from local residents to NRI devotees — find their dream homes, investment plots, and commercial spaces in Mathura, Vrindavan, Barsana, and Govardhan.

Our promise is simple: transparent dealings, RERA-compliant listings, and a deeply personalized service that treats every client as a member of our Braj family.
